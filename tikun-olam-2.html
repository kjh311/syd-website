Going Green at TBT

In conjunction with the City of Bellevue, TBT has completed its pledge to “Refresh Our Recycling!” You can click on the image to see our name on the official Bellevue website. Thank you for partnering with us in sustainability!

What are those green barrels?

Compost Bins!

Those green barrels alongside the trash and recycling bins in TBT’s kitchen and social hall are compost bins. You can throw all of your food waste in them. TBT’s disposable plates, cups, napkins, and utensils are all compostable, so you can dispose of them in the compost bins too. Bal tashchit, “do not waste,” is an important Jewish principle, and composting will help all of us abide by it.
