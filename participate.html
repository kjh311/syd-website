Participating in our services is a fantastic way to serve with both time and talent. From welcoming guests and old friends to showcasing your artistic gifts or your love of Torah, there are many ways to be involved.


Hospitality

Belonging to a synagogue is about creating community, and our Hospitality Team is an important way in which you can contribute to our community’s Beit Tefilah, house of prayer. Our Hospitality Team enhances our community’s prayer services by warmly greeting congregants and guests; handing out prayer books, programs, kippot, tallitot, and other worship items; and answering general questions.

Greeters are needed for all of our gatherings. Greeters are scheduled well in advance a few times each year. If you are interested in contributing to our community as part of the Hospitality Team, please contact our office at contact@templebnaitorah.org or call (425) 603-9677.

Davening Team

Do you love Jewish music?  Do you sing or play an instrument?  Temple B'nai Torah is looking for intermediate to advanced musicians to participate in t'filah on Shabbat and holidays. As a team with other talented congregants, you will learn about our sacred liturgy, discover new Jewish music and co-create a powerful  and transformative prayer experience unlike any other in the Pacific Northwest! If you would like to audition for our Davening Team, please contact our artists-in-residence, Josh Niehaus at jniehaus@templebnaitorah.org

Torah Reading

Temple B’nai Torah encourages all congregants to actively engage with our Torah, from reading, chanting, blessing and handling the Torah during our services.  If you would like to participate in our Saturday morning Torah readings on a regular basis, please email contact@templebnaitorah.com and you will be put in direct contact with one of our rabbis.
