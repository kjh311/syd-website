Periodically through the year, we create services specifically designed to help us be more fully alive to God’s presence in the world…to build a life that is God-centered, as we reclaim the unique spirituality of Judaism, deeply embedded in social consciousness. Join us as our worship re-energizes Jewish piety by making it more emotionally satisfying, inclusive, experimental, experiential, and compelling, reconnecting to God through our hearts and our minds, through openness to a Jewish life that emphasizes joy and celebration of the grandeur of the universe, and through studying the deepest spiritual truths of the Jewish people. Join us at TBT for a unique worship experience…a Soul Shabbat…where we can set aside the rubric prayers of the past and seek deeper meaning in our Shabbat moments. Come help us create a sacred moment in time…Let’s pray!
