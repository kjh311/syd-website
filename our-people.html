Who We Are

Temple B’nai Torah’s leadership is young and old, single and married, new to temple management and steeped in decades of service to congregational families, organizations and business entities. It’s this mix, this merging of divergent experience and views into one cohesive whole, that makes Temple B’nai Torah the dynamic and innovative congregation it is today. We have a Board of Directors and a strong committee structure. Our professional administrators and staff are experienced, committed to the Temple community, and sensitive to the needs and concerns of the congregation. Get to know us and get involved.
