From Jews and Brews to summer bar-be-cues, Shabbat in the Park, hiking, gardening, game nights and cooking together, our TBT  family knows how to have fun. Our social programs evolve to meet the needs of our membership. We are a place where when someone asks: Do you have a .....? The answer is almost always: not yet, would you like to start one?

For those who enjoy planning and doing the work of the congregation, we welcome participation on the temple's many leadership committees.

And our active and vibrant Sisterhood creates social and educational program for all adults, not just those who identify as women.
