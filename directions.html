Directions to Temple B’nai Torah

Temple B’nai Torah is located at 15727 NE 4th Street in Bellevue, Washington, just a few blocks south of the Crossroads Shopping Center and next door to the Jewish Day School.

 

From 520:

Take the NE 148th Street Exit – South.

Take a left on 8th Street (at the Super Supplements).

Take a right on 156th Avenue (at the Bartell’s).

Take a left on NE 4th Street.

Take a right into the parking lot just a block from NE 4th and 156th.

 

From I-90, traveling east (away from Seattle):

Take the 148th Ave Exit – North (Exit 11B).

Merge onto 148th Ave SE

Stay on 148th going north for several miles.

Turn right on Main Street

Turn left on 156th Avenue

Turn right on NE 4th Street.

Take a right into the parking lot just a block from NE 4th and 156th.

 

From I-90, traveling west (toward Seattle):

Take the 156th Avenue Exit – North (Exit 11).

Follow signs through exit ramp to 156th Ave. SE. Stay on 156th going north for several miles.

Take a right on NE 4th Street.

Take a right into the parking lot just a block from NE 4th and 156th.
