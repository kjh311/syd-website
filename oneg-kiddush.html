One of the easiest ways for you to get involved in services – is to sponsor our community gatherings on Friday evening or Saturday afternoon. Read the details below, then use the contact form to get in touch with Marianne to select a date! If you are unsure of a date to sponsor, please call our office at 425.603.9677 to see which dates we have available. Thank you!

 
Oneg Sponsors

Cost to sponsor a Shabbat Oneg: $120

If you wish to provide the Oneg yourself:

  • Baked goods: 12 dozen (~150) pieces

  • Fruit Tray

  • Pay $20 to cover ancillary costs such as tea, coffee, and serving supplies

  • Help with clean up.

Kiddush Luncheon Sponsors

Cost to sponsor a Shabbat Kiddush: $150

If you wish to provide the Kiddush Luncheon yourself:

  • Provide reasonable food & beverage for ~40 people

  • Help with clean up.
