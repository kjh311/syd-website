Temple B’nai Torah Judaica Store

 

Find everything you need for B’nai Mitzvah, High Holy Days, Passover, Chanukah, and Shabbat: cards, mezuzot, tallitot, kippot, jewelry, serving dishes, artwork, books, and so much more! All gift shop proceeds go towards camp scholarships.

Stop by for New Year cards, honey pots & dippers, and other beautiful and high-quality High Holy Day items!

Open during Temple business hours by arrangement with our Temple Office Staff:

Tuesday – Friday 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM

The Sisterhood Judaica Gift Shop is managed and staffed by Sisterhood as a service to Temple B’nai Torah and the community. Our shop is small – think of us as a Judaica boutique – but we’re well stocked! The best selection of tallitot and kippot in the Seattle area, for starters, and a broad range of holiday, ritual, simcha and Israeli art goods as well. Think of your Sisterhood Judaica Shop for special birthday gifts, hostess gifts, thank you gifts and feel good about yourself gifts too!

Voted Best Judaica Shop in the Seattle area by the JTNEWS.

Register: Be sure to register your upcoming B’nai Mitzvah, weddings, confirmations and baby namings with us, and then tell friends and family about it.

Become a Judaica Shop Volunteer: It’s a great place to meet and greet Temple members – make new friends, and get 15% off on all your shop purchases too! 

For your shopping convenience, we accept VISA and MasterCard.
