Join us as we Lift Up Our Community in Worship!

At Temple B’nai Torah, our mission is to create an Inclusive, Welcoming, Sacred Jewish Home.

 

Kabbalat Shabbat

Fridays, 6:30 PM

Every Friday night, we offer a Shabbat service led by our clergy and gifted musicians. This is a time for reflection on the week past and the week ahead. We are currently holding virtual services and you can join us via livestream or Facebook.

 

Shabbat Services

Saturdays, 10:30 AM

Join us on Saturday mornings for a time of music and celebration of the Torah. On Saturday morning we celebrate our B’nai Mitzvot and we also gather for Torah Study and worship. Saturday morning services are currently being held via Zoom. Please email contact@templebnaitorah.org for the Zoom link.

 

Tot Shabbat

Our Tot Shabbat program for parents and children 0-5 welcomes Shabbat with singing, dancing, stories, and blessings. Join Rabbi Molly Weisel and Josh Niehaus for a virtual Tot Shabbat on the first Friday evening each month from 5:30-6 pm and on the third Saturday from 9:30-10 am. Email contact@templebnaitorah.org for the Zoom link.
