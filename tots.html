Solomike History

In 2009, Temple B’nai Torah saw the unexpected passing of Mike “Solomike” Negrin, a beloved and committed member of our community. Mike generously left Temple B’nai Torah a bequest to strengthen our temple in the future – a fund dedicated to the establishment of an early childhood center. Through this fund, we hope to engage new families and children in Jewish communal life through a rewarding and enriching educational program.

For questions please contact us at totshabbat@templebnaitorah.org

Tot Shabbat & PJ Havdalah

Our Tot Shabbat program for parents and children 0-5 welcomes Shabbat with singing, dancing, stories, and blessings. Join Cantor David Serkin-Poole and Chava Mirel for this free drop-in program, no RSVP required!

We also offer Pajama Havdalah programs throughout the year with stories, snacks, activities and havdalah.
