Summertime Shabbat Services... Get Out in the Sun!

Shabbat in the Park returns as we once again get out from behind our walls to bring Jewish life outside in a fun and sacred way. Bring your picnic dinner and blanket or chairs starting at 5:30 PM. Schmooze and relax before an amazing service at 6 PM. Join neighbors, make new friends and welcome Shabbat in the beauty of creation with Temple B’nai Torah. All ages welcome!

Picnic Gathering: 5:30 PM Services: 6 PM
