Mitzvah Corps Needs You!

Mitzvah Corps of Temple B’nai Torah is an all-volunteer group made up of fabulous folks from the Temple community who help our fellow members and their families in all sorts of ways. Volunteers may pick up groceries for an ailing member, visit a home-bound senior, call a member who is recovering from surgery or drive someone to services or a medical appointment if they are not well enough to do so themselves. There are all kinds of ways to help out, and volunteers can give a little or a lot. It’s up to you.

If you need help yourself, be sure to call us so we can connect you with the help you need.

Contact Robin Negrin at mitzvahcorps@templebnaitorah.org or call the Temple office at (425) 603-9677.
