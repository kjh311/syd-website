Time, Talent or Treasure…what will you offer to Lift Up Your Community?

There are many ways you can support your Temple. We depend on a diverse group of wonderful and invaluable volunteers. If you would like information on volunteering, click here, or please contact us. Donations don’t only come via check. Time given has proven to be integral to TBT’s success.

However, membership dues only make up 50% of our budget. We also depend on donations to allow us to grow our future generations and provide for our community — all ages, all genders, all races, all abilities. Here are ways you can support your Temple.
